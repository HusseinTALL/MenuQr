# Hotel Room Service - Guest Guide

## Welcome to Room Service

Order delicious meals and beverages delivered directly to your room using your smartphone. Simply scan the QR code in your room to get started!

---

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Scan the QR Code

Look for the QR code placed in your room (usually on the desk, nightstand, or menu card). Open your phone's camera and point it at the QR code.

### Step 2: Authenticate

Enter your room PIN or access code:
- **Room PIN**: The 4-digit code provided at check-in
- **Access Code**: The unique code on your key card envelope

### Step 3: Browse & Order

Browse the menu, add items to your cart, and place your order. Your food will be delivered to your room!

---

## Detailed Instructions

### Scanning the QR Code

1. Open the camera app on your smartphone
2. Point it at the QR code until a link appears
3. Tap the link to open the room service menu
4. If the link doesn't appear, try a QR code scanner app

**Tip**: Make sure you have a stable internet connection (WiFi or mobile data).

### Authentication Options

#### Option A: Room PIN

1. Select "Enter Room PIN"
2. Enter the 4-digit PIN provided at check-in
3. Tap "Verify"

**Forgot your PIN?** Contact the front desk to receive a new PIN.

#### Option B: Access Code

1. Select "Enter Access Code"
2. Enter the code printed on your key card envelope
3. Tap "Verify"

### Order Status

Track your order in real-time:

| Status | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Pending** | Order received, awaiting confirmation |
| **Confirmed** | Kitchen has accepted your order |
| **Preparing** | Your food is being prepared |

Your confirmation shows an estimated delivery time based on:

- Your order is confirmed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: What are the room service hours?**
A: Room service is typically available from 6:00 to 23:00. Check the app for exact hours.

**Q: Is there a minimum order amount?**
A: This varies by hotel. The minimum (if any) is displayed before checkout.

**Q: How long will my order take?**
A: Delivery times depend on what you order. Estimated time is shown at checkout.

### Authentication

**Q: I forgot my room PIN, what do I do?**
A: Contact the front desk. They can reset your PIN or provide your access code.

**Q: Can I order from multiple devices?**
A: Yes, you can authenticate on multiple devices with the same PIN/code.

**Q: My PIN isn't working, what's wrong?**
A: Make sure you're entering the correct room number and PIN. If problems persist, contact the front desk.

### Orders

**Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?**
A: Contact the front desk immediately. Changes may be possible if preparation hasn't started.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Orders can be cancelled before they start preparing. Contact the front desk or use the app.

**Q: What if I'm not in my room when the order arrives?**
A: Staff will try to contact you. If unreachable, they may leave the order at your door or return later.

### Payment

**Q: Will room charges appear on my final bill?**
A: Yes, all room charges are added to your hotel bill and settled at checkout.

**Q: Is tipping included?**
A: The service charge is included. Additional tips can be given at your discretion.

**Q: Can I use a different payment method?**
A: Yes, you can choose card or cash on delivery at checkout.

### Dietary & Allergies

**Q: Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?**
A: Yes! Use the special instructions field or speak with the front desk for specific requirements.

**Q: Are allergen ingredients clearly marked?**
A: Yes, all major allergens are displayed on each dish. For severe allergies, please call the kitchen.
